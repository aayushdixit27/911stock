## V2

Imagine winning a hackathon prize for a stock market project.

If you're following a stock on CNBC, it's already too late when it tanks (or shoots up). But what if you could skip the mental work of refreshing X posts and news feeds -- and just get a call from an AI telling you when a CEO dumps his stock?

That's what me and my partner Big built at the Deep Agents Hackathon (RSAC 2026).

Using Bland AI, our agent monitors the market and news in real time and calls you when a stock turns bearish. You get an Auth0 push notification on your phone (NO TRADES DONE AUTOMATICALLY!) and have the choice to approve the trade yourself.

Just tell us which stocks you want to follow. Save hundreds -- or thousands -- on your next transaction.

Your friend calls you the next day: "I lost so much money!"

You say: "I didn't. I used 911Stock."

---

Huge thanks to the team at TrueFoundry for buying into the vision.

I built the frontend in Next.js -- real-time news timeline, live stock prices, and a three-screen demo flow that walks you from calm markets to crisis to trade execution. Big handled the backend: Claude for analysis, Bland for voice, Ghost DB for agent memory, Auth0 CIBA for trade authorization.

At Votal, I spend my days telling people about our AI security and red-teaming products. At this hackathon, I spent the day doing UX research -- walking up to strangers and asking "Would you use this today?" and "What would make you say, 'I need this right now?'" The answers ranged from "100% yes, no-brainer" to "this sounds like a scam." Both made the product better.

We ran into every hiccup imaginable -- a Vercel 404 right before demo time, Auth0 integration issues, users who told us it wasn't valuable. We powered through.

And we won a $300 prize from TrueFoundry!
