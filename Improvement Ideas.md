**2. Let the Judge Call IN**                                  

  Right now: the agent calls out. One-directional.

  **Upgrade:** After the outbound call demo, hand the judge your phone and say: "You can also call the agent. Ask it anything about your portfolio."                     

  Judge calls the number. Agent picks up: _"Hey, I'm your Stock Guardian. You're watching NVDA, TSLA, and AAPL. What do you want to know?"_                            

  Judge: "What's happening with Tesla?"                                                                                                                              

  Agent: _"Tesla's been quiet today. No insider transactions this week. The last notable event was the CEO purchasing 50,000 shares on March 12th — that's actually a_ 

  _bullish signal. Nothing urgent for you right now."_        

  **Why this is a FUCK YES moment:** The judge is now USING the product. Not watching a demo. Using it. That's the difference between "I can see how someone would use   

  this" and "give me this right now." The playbook calls this unprompted demand — when they start interacting without being asked.

  **Build cost:** Small. Bland supports inbound calls. You add a second Bland agent with the user's watchlist injected as context. Maybe 30 extra minutes of work.
